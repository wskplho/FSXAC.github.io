INFORMATION

All manufactured products and services require a legal document called a blueprint or drawing. 
 

Essentially every object or device from a thumbtack to a nuclear power plant must have a plan. 
 

As long as there is research and manufacturing, there will always be a need for Mechanical Computer Aided Drafting skills.   
 

Highly detailed and accurate drawings demand both two-dimensional and three-dimensional drafting skills.
